General explanation about Physics major:
The Physics major is all about understanding how the universe works.
You'll study everything from tiny atoms to giant galaxies, learning about forces, energy, and matter.
It's a challenging major that makes you a great problem-solver and a very logical thinker.
Physics graduates can go into research, engineering, teaching, or even jobs that need strong analytical skills, like in finance.

General xplanation about computer science major:

Computer Science is about computers and how to make them do amazing things.
You'll learn how to write code, design programs, and understand the logic behind all technology.
It's a field that's always changing and has tons of job opportunities.
Computer Science graduates are in high demand everywhere, working as software developers, data experts, and helping to create the next big tech innovations.
