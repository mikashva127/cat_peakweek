Universty-High school academy
High School Academy is a program for outstanding students from the ninth grade onwards, which allows them to take academic courses at the Open University during high school.
Students can earn credits towards a bachelor's degree, and convert part of their matriculation exams into academic courses.
Participation: Is conditional on school approval and meeting the threshold requirements.
The goal of the program: to deepen knowledge, be exposed to academic studies and prepare for higher education.
